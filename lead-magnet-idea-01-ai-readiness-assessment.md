# Lead Magnet Idea #01 — AI Readiness Assessment for Engineering Firms

## One-liner
A 3–5 minute assessment that tells engineering companies (EPC/Oil & Gas/Utilities) **how ready they are for AI automation**, what they should do next, and offers a free consultation.

## Why it works (business angle)
- High-intent: People taking an assessment are already thinking about transformation.
- Gives you segmentation: you can tag leads by maturity level.
- Lets KU Automation pitch the *next step* with a specific plan.

## Target audience
- EPC contractors
- Owner/operators (oil & gas, utilities)
- Maintenance contractors
- Engineering consultancies

## Format
- 10–12 questions
- Multiple-choice (A/B/C/D)
- Output: score + maturity tier + recommended next actions

## Scoring tiers
- **0–25: Stage 1 — Ad hoc**
- **26–50: Stage 2 — Repeatable**
- **51–75: Stage 3 — Managed**
- **76–100: Stage 4 — Optimized**

## Questions (draft)
1. **Where is your engineering/project data stored?**
   - A) Mostly email/WhatsApp/individual laptops
   - B) Shared drive / SharePoint, inconsistent naming
   - C) Document control system, but lots of manual work
   - D) Document control + structured metadata + governance

2. **How often do you re-enter the same data across documents?**
   - A) Constantly
   - B) Weekly
   - C) Occasionally
   - D) Rarely

3. **Do you have standard templates for RFQs / ITPs / MDR / datasheets?**
   - A) No
   - B) Some
   - C) Most
   - D) Fully standardized + version controlled

4. **How do you review vendor datasheets and compliance?**
   - A) Manual only
   - B) Manual with checklists
   - C) Some automation (Excel macros, scripts)
   - D) Automated extraction + compliance checking

5. **How do you track action items from meetings and site queries?**
   - A) Notes only
   - B) Excel
   - C) Dedicated tool but not consistent
   - D) Tool + automation + reminders

6. **How do you classify and find documents?**
   - A) Search by file name
   - B) Folder structure
   - C) Metadata tags sometimes
   - D) Enforced metadata + search + OCR

7. **Are your key systems integrated (email, DMS, ERP, procurement)?**
   - A) No
   - B) Small integrations
   - C) Some APIs used
   - D) Integrated workflows

8. **Do you have an internal automation/AI champion or team?**
   - A) No
   - B) One person occasionally
   - C) Small team
   - D) Dedicated function

9. **What’s your biggest blocker for automation?**
   - A) No time
   - B) No data quality
   - C) No budget
   - D) Not sure where to start

10. **Which best describes leadership support?**
   - A) Not interested
   - B) Curious
   - C) Supportive
   - D) Actively funding

## Results page copy (draft)
- Your score: **X/100**
- Your stage: **Stage Y — <name>**
- Recommended next steps (3 bullets)
- Suggested quick wins (2 bullets)
- CTA: **Book a 30-min AI Readiness Call** (Calendly)

- Day 0: results + quick wins
- Day 2: case study teaser (or “what good looks like”)
- Day 5: offer: 2-week pilot
