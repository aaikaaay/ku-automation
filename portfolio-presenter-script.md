## Q&A PREPARATION

### Likely Questions & Suggested Answers

**Q: "How accurate is the AI?"**
> "For document extraction, we typically see 85-95% accuracy on first pass. The key is that it dramatically reduces time — even if an engineer needs to review and correct 10% of outputs, they're still saving 70%+ of their time compared to starting from scratch."

**Q: "What if our documents are in unusual formats?"**
> "Our system handles PDFs, images, scanned documents, and multi-page files. During the PoC phase, we test on your actual documents to ensure compatibility. If we encounter edge cases, we tune the models."

**Q: "How long does implementation take?"**
> "For a pilot, typically 4 weeks. For full production deployment, 6-8 weeks including training. Cloud SaaS is fastest — you could be running within days. On-premise takes longer due to infrastructure setup."

**Q: "What happens to our data?"**
> "By default, documents are processed and immediately deleted — zero retention. We never use client data to train models for other clients. If you need retention for audit purposes, that's configurable with your own encryption keys."

**Q: "Can this integrate with [specific system]?"**
> "We have native integrations with SharePoint, SAP, AVEVA, and Hexagon. For other systems, we offer a REST API that your IT team can use to build custom integrations. We've integrated with most major engineering document management systems."

**Q: "What's the typical ROI?"**
> "Most clients see payback within 4 weeks. The calculation is straightforward: if a senior engineer costs £80/hour and we save them 20 hours per week, that's £1,600/week or £83,000/year — far exceeding the cost of most deployments."

**Q: "Do you have references we can speak to?"**
> "Yes, we can arrange reference calls with existing clients in similar industries. We'll need an NDA in place first to protect their confidentiality, but we're happy to facilitate that."
