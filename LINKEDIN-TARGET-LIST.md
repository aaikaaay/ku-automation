## Research Methodology

All prospects were identified through structured web searches using `site:linkedin.com/in` queries combined with company names, job titles, and geographies. Each name was cross-referenced against at least one public source (LinkedIn search result snippet, company website, conference page, or news article). Profiles confirmed as gated with no secondary verification were not included. Where a specific individual could not be verified at a target company, a verified substitute at the same company or an equivalent company in the same tier was selected.

**Search patterns used:**
- `site:linkedin.com/in "Principal Piping Engineer" ADNOC`
- `site:linkedin.com/in "Lead Mechanical Engineer" McDermott International`
- `"Engineering Manager" [Company] oil gas UAE LinkedIn`
- `"Lead Piping Engineer" Technip Energies Qatar`
- `site:linkedin.com/in "Principal Process Engineer" Saipem`
- `"small engineering consultancy" founder oil gas UAE OR Aberdeen LinkedIn`
- Company team pages, About/Team pages for Cohort C firms

**Geographic distribution achieved:**
- Tier 1 (UAE, Saudi Arabia, Qatar, Kuwait, Oman, UK, Houston/Texas, Norway, Netherlands): ~60%
- Tier 2 (Malaysia, Singapore, India, Australia, Canada, Egypt, Nigeria, Brazil): ~40%

## USAGE NOTES

### How to Use the Personalisation Hook in the Connection Request

LinkedIn connection requests are capped at **200–300 characters**. Use the personalisation hook to write a **specific, human connection note** — NOT a generic sales pitch.

**Formula:**
> "Hi [Name] — saw your work on [personalisation hook detail]. I'm building a similar niche — [one-line about KU Automation]. Would love to connect with a fellow [piping/process/structural] professional."

**Examples:**

- For Kunio Nagai (Chiyoda): *"Hi Kunio — noticed your work developing piping design algorithms at Chiyoda. I'm doing something adjacent — AI-assisted engineering review on P&IDs and isos. Fellow piping automation thinker — would love to connect."*

- For Rick Carroll (DOCAN): *"Hi Rick — fellow CEng in the engineering review space. You built DOCAN on pipe stress and FFS consulting; I'm applying AI to deliverable review for oil & gas operators. Would love to connect and compare notes."*

- For Shripad Sheth (ADNOC PMC): *"Hi Shripad — saw your PMC piping lead role on ADNOC's NEB Asset. I'm based in UAE too — building AI-assisted principal-engineer review for piping deliverables. Would be great to connect."*

**Key rule:** Use **one specific, accurate detail** from the hook. Do not fabricate or exaggerate. If the profile is gated when you visit, use the search keyword to locate them and use the role/company as the hook.
