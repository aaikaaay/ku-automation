5. Proposal Structure

  1. Executive Summary - High-level overview, key benefits, investment summary
  2. Understanding of Requirements - Demonstrate you understand their needs
  3. Proposed Solution - What you will deliver and how
  4. Scope of Work - Detailed breakdown of deliverables
  5. Timeline & Milestones - Project schedule with key dates
  6. Team & Expertise - Who will work on the project, credentials
  7. Investment - Pricing with breakdown, payment terms
  8. Terms & Conditions - Assumptions, exclusions, validity
  9. Why KU Automation - Differentiators, relevant experience
  10. Next Steps - Call to action, how to proceed

6. Pricing Calculation Process

  1. Estimate Effort: Break down into tasks, estimate hours/days for each
  2. Apply Rates: Use appropriate rate card rates
  3. Add Contingency: 15-25% for fixed-price projects
  4. Consider Value: Adjust based on value delivered to client
  5. Market Check: Ensure competitive while maintaining margin
  6. Internal Review: Principal approval for projects >$25,000

8. Payment Terms

8.1 Standard Payment Schedule

  • Deposit: 50% upon contract signing
  • Progress Payment: 25% at mid-project milestone
  • Final Payment: 25% upon delivery and acceptance

8.2 Alternative Schedules

  • Monthly billing for T&M projects
  • Monthly in advance for retainers
  • Custom schedules for large projects (with approval)

8.3 Payment Terms

  • Invoices due within 14 days
  • Late payment: 1.5% interest per month
  • Work paused if payments 30+ days overdue
