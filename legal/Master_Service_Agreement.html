1. Services

1.1 Provider agrees to provide AI automation consulting and development services as described in one or more Statements of Work ("SOW") executed under this Agreement.

1.2 Services may include, but are not limited to:

  1. AI-powered document processing and data extraction
  2. Workflow automation design and implementation
  3. Chatbot and virtual assistant development
  4. Custom AI solution development
  5. System integration and API development
  6. Training and knowledge transfer
  7. Ongoing support and maintenance

1.3 Each SOW shall specify the scope, deliverables, timeline, and fees for the particular engagement.

2. Statements of Work

2.1 All Services shall be performed pursuant to a SOW signed by both parties.

2.2 Each SOW shall include: (a) description of Services; (b) deliverables; (c) timeline and milestones; (d) fees and payment schedule; (e) acceptance criteria; (f) any project-specific terms.

2.3 In case of conflict between this Agreement and a SOW, the terms of the SOW shall prevail for that specific engagement.

3. Fees and Payment

3.1 Client shall pay Provider the fees specified in each SOW.

3.2 Unless otherwise specified in a SOW, payment terms are:

  1. 50% deposit upon SOW execution
  2. 25% upon completion of development phase
  3. 25% upon final delivery and acceptance

3.3 Invoices are due within 14 days of receipt.

3.4 Late payments shall incur interest at 1.5% per month.

3.5 All fees are exclusive of applicable taxes, which shall be Client's responsibility.

4. Client Obligations

4.1 Client shall:

  1. Provide timely access to necessary information, data, and systems
  2. Designate a project contact with authority to make decisions
  3. Review and approve deliverables within agreed timeframes
  4. Provide feedback promptly when requested
  5. Ensure compliance with data protection laws for data provided

4.2 Delays caused by Client may result in timeline adjustments and additional fees.

5. Intellectual Property

5.1 Provider IP: All pre-existing intellectual property, methodologies, tools, frameworks, and general knowledge developed by Provider prior to or independent of this Agreement remain Provider's property.

5.2 Client IP: All Client data, materials, and pre-existing IP remain Client's property.

5.3 Project Deliverables: Upon full payment, Client receives a perpetual, non-exclusive license to use deliverables specifically created for Client under each SOW. Provider retains ownership of underlying methodologies and reusable components.

5.4 Custom Development: Where a SOW specifies work-for-hire or full IP transfer, ownership shall transfer to Client upon full payment, subject to Provider's retained license to use general knowledge and methodologies.

6. Confidentiality

6.1 Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality of proprietary information disclosed during the engagement.

6.2 If a separate NDA has been executed, its terms shall govern confidentiality obligations.

6.3 Confidentiality obligations survive termination for five (5) years.

7.2 Provider shall implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures.

7.3 Client warrants that it has obtained necessary consents for any personal data provided to Provider.

8.4 Provider does not guarantee specific business outcomes from AI implementations.

9.2 IN NO EVENT SHALL EITHER PARTY BE LIABLE FOR INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, OR CONSEQUENTIAL DAMAGES.

9.3 These limitations shall not apply to breaches of confidentiality, intellectual property infringement, or gross negligence.

10. Term and Termination

10.1 This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues until terminated.

10.2 Either party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days written notice.

10.3 Either party may terminate immediately upon material breach that remains uncured for 15 days after written notice.

10.4 Upon termination:

  1. Client shall pay for all Services rendered through termination date
  2. Provider shall deliver all completed work product
  3. Each party shall return or destroy confidential information

11.1 Changes to scope must be documented in a written Change Request.

11.2 Provider shall assess impact on timeline and fees.

11.3 No changes shall be implemented without mutual written agreement.
