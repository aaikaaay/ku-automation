1. Purpose

This SOP establishes a standardized process for onboarding new clients to ensure consistent, professional, and efficient client intake across all engagements. It ensures all necessary information is collected, expectations are set, and the foundation for a successful project is established.

2. Scope

This procedure applies to all new client engagements for AI automation services, including:

  • Document processing and data extraction projects
  • Workflow automation implementations
  • Chatbot and virtual assistant development
  • Custom AI solution development
  • Consulting and advisory services

4. Onboarding Process

1Initial Contact & Qualification (Day 1)

  • Receive inquiry via website, email, or referral
  • Respond within 4 business hours
  • Conduct initial qualification call (15-30 minutes)
  • Assess fit: industry, project scope, budget alignment
  • Document in CRM/project management system

2Discovery Meeting (Day 2-5)

  • Schedule comprehensive discovery session (60-90 minutes)
  • Use Discovery Questionnaire Template
  • Understand current pain points and processes
  • Identify automation opportunities
  • Document technical requirements and constraints
  • Identify key stakeholders and decision-makers

3NDA Execution (Day 3-7)

  • Send NDA for review and signature
  • Use DocuSign or equivalent for electronic signatures
  • Store executed NDA in secure client folder
  • Confirm receipt and countersign

4Proposal Development (Day 5-10)

  • Develop detailed proposal including:
    • Executive summary
    • Scope of work
    • Technical approach
    • Timeline and milestones
    • Investment and payment terms
    • Assumptions and exclusions
  • Internal review and approval
  • Present proposal to client

5Contract Execution (Day 10-15)

  • Send Master Service Agreement (MSA) or Statement of Work (SOW)
  • Address any client questions or requested modifications
  • Obtain signatures from all parties
  • Process initial payment/deposit
  • Issue payment confirmation

6Project Kickoff (Day 15-20)

  • Schedule kickoff meeting with all stakeholders
  • Create project workspace (ClickUp/shared folders)
  • Set up communication channels (Slack/Teams/Email)
  • Share project timeline and deliverables
  • Establish reporting schedule
  • Request access to necessary systems/data

5. Required Documentation Checklist

8. Tools & Systems

  • CRM: ClickUp / HubSpot for client tracking

9. Quality Standards

  • All client communications must be professional and timely
  • All documents must use approved KU Automation templates
  • All sensitive information must be stored securely
  • Client satisfaction check after kickoff meeting
⚠️ Important: Never begin work on a project until the NDA and contract are fully executed and initial payment is received (where applicable).
