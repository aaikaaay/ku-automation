## Checklist Content

### 🔍 AI READINESS ASSESSMENT
*Score yourself: Give 1 point for each "Yes"*

---

#### DATA & DOCUMENTS

☐ **1. Digital Documents**
We have most project documents in digital format (PDFs, Word, Excel) rather than paper-only.

☐ **2. Consistent Naming**
Our files follow a somewhat consistent naming convention or folder structure.

☐ **3. Accessible Archives**
Past project data is stored where it can be searched/accessed (not lost on old hard drives).

---

#### PROCESSES & PAIN POINTS

☐ **4. Repetitive Reports**
We generate similar reports repeatedly (weekly status, monthly compliance, project summaries).

☐ **5. Manual Data Entry**
Staff spend significant time copying data between systems or documents.

☐ **6. Document Review Bottlenecks**
Technical document review (drawings, specs, RFPs) creates delays in our workflow.

---

#### TEAM & CULTURE

☐ **7. Tech-Open Leadership**
Management is open to adopting new technology if it saves time/money.

☐ **8. Process Documentation**
We have documented (or could document) our key workflows and procedures.

☐ **9. One Champion**
At least one person on our team is excited about AI/automation possibilities.

---

#### BUSINESS DRIVERS

☐ **10. Clear Pain Point**
We can identify at least one process that costs us significant time, money, or frustration.

---

## Scoring Guide

**8-10 Points: AI-Ready 🚀**
Your company has strong foundations for AI automation. You could see ROI within 60-90 days of implementation.

**5-7 Points: Almost There 🔧**
Minor groundwork needed. A quick data/process audit could prepare you for high-impact automation.

**2-4 Points: Foundation Phase 🏗️**
Start with digitization and process documentation. AI benefits are 6-12 months away but achievable.

**0-1 Points: Early Stage 📋**
Focus on basic digital infrastructure first. We can help you build the foundation.
