## A note before you start

1. Shows prospects you're a real, structured business (not a one-person side project)

## 12. Content Strategy for the Company Page

This is **separate** from the personal-profile posting strategy in `LINKEDIN-MARKETING-PLAYBOOK.md`. Different cadence, different content mix.

### Cadence
- **2 posts per week** on the Company Page (Tue + Thu, 9-11am Dubai)
- Personal profile = 3 posts per week
- When personal post performs well, **reshare from Company Page 24-48 hours later** with a different intro line

### Content mix (per 8 posts / month)
- 2× product / service announcements
- 2× "anatomy of a finding" teardowns (from the AI reviews)
- 2× industry/standards explainers (no CTA — pure value)
- 1× customer/use case (synthetic until you have real ones)
- 1× behind-the-scenes / vision

### Posting from Company Page vs personal profile
LinkedIn organic reach for Company Page posts is typically **2-5% of followers**. Personal profile reach is **10-30%**. So:

- **Important announcements** (new product, milestone) → post BOTH from personal first, then Company Page, with personal resharing Company Page post 24h later
- **Value content** (teardowns, explainers) → post from personal only, reshare to Company Page later if it does well
- **Brand-y content** (we got featured in X, hiring announcements) → Company Page only
